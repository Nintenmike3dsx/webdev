Gameplay:

Lethal Company contains a whopping 12 moons to explore! The game is highly praised by both players and reviewers. Users on PC are able to play Lethal company together through Steam. They use their keyboard and mouse to control their character. The game has you looting different plants to get money for the company. You want to meet your quota ASAP.

When starting the game your crew will begin on a ship. Using the ship's computer, you can travel to the different planets.

Once you arrive at the selected moon, you have the day on the planet to loot and gather as much scrap as you can to meet your quota. As you continue playing, you get to more hostile moons and more valuable loot.

The Monsters Within:

Lethal Company features more than 20 different monsters. Each monster you encounter is added to your database.You can read information about the monster to learn about its weaknesses in order to defeat it. Once you are killed, you are eliminated for the rest of the day.
So it is very important that you stay alive!
