Lethal Company: An Employee Review

Introduction:

Lethal Company is a co-op horror game where you and your friends must scavenge for loot on alien planets. The game was developed by a single indie dev, Zeekerss and released on October 23rd, 2026.

Gameplay:

Lethal Company contains a whopping 12 moons to explore! The game is highly praised by both players and reviewers. Users on PCare able to play Lethal company together through Steam and other gaming platforms. They use their keyboard and mouse to control their character.
